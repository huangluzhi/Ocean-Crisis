Game control (it is recommended that two people play unless you are confident in your left and right brain coordination):
Upper w
Lower S
Left a
Right d
Bomb space bar
Shooting left mouse button
Variable submarine J (only in infinite mode)
Change ship K (only in infinite mode)
Variable plane L (only in infinite mode)
Tips:
The higher the explosive speed, the higher the score of the enemy aircraft or submarine
It takes a certain score to launch the shell. Please aim and then start: -)
The final score of the game must be greater than 0 to be included in the ranking
In infinite mode:
Be careful not to blow up marine life, but when you become a submarine, you can catch it as a food supply.
When it becomes an aircraft or submarine, the energy consumed will increase greatly, and the fraction will decrease slowly with time.
Enjoy!
PS: you can open two windows and play at the same time: -)

游戏控制(建议两人玩除非你对自己左右脑配合足够自信）：
上                   w
下                   s
左                   a
右                   d
投弹                空格键
射击                鼠标左键
变潜艇             j（仅在无尽模式中）
变船                k（仅在无尽模式中）
变飞机             l（仅在无尽模式中）

Tips:
炸毁速度越高的敌机或潜艇得到分数越高
发射炮弹需要消耗一定分数，请瞄准再下手 :-)
游戏最终得分至少要大于0才能计入排行榜
在无尽模式中：
注意别炸到海洋生物，但变成潜艇时可以捕捉作为食物补给
变成飞机或潜艇时，消耗的能量会大大增高，分数会随时间慢慢减少

Enjoy!

ps:你可以同时开两个窗口然后两个同时玩 :-)
